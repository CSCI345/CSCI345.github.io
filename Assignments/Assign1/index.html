CSCI 345: Computers and the Physical World (Spring 2019, Oberlin Collegel)

CSCI 345: Assignment 1

Project Ideas
Due: 10PM on Monday, February 10th

Group Project

In this class, you will work on a semester long group project exploring how computers can interact with the physical world. Your project can be based around arduinos, any microcontroller, or a raspberry pi. It must take in input from multiple sensors (possibly including the internet), and display output on something other than a screen. You can create a useful object, an art project, or any other interactive system.

Get Inspired

The goal of this assignment is for you to think about what you would like to do for your group project. With that in mind, please look through the following websites and pick three projects you feel inspired by:
  • Adafruit
  • Sparkfun
  • Instructables
  • Guthman Instrument Competition
  • Guthman Videos
  • NIME conference (look in past NIME conference proceedings)
  • MOCO conference (look in past MOCO conference proceedings)

Post

Go to the piazza forum for this class, and leave a comment under the "Assignment 1" note with links to the three projects you like, and a 3-5 word description of each project. Write a couple of sentences about what kind of project you would like to work on, e.g. "I'd like to do something with LEDs and sewable circuits. Something like a jacket that lights up when you move." Please read other people's posts to see if there are people interested in similar projects you could possibly work with.
